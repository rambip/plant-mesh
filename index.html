Commands

For now, the commands to interact with the tree are:
  • Enter: show or hide tree
  • A: toggle animation
  • Right Arrow or drag: turn counter-clockwise
  • Left Arrow or drag: turn clockwise
  • Plus or scroll: Zoom in
  • Minus or scroll: Zoom out
  • W: toggle wireframe (mesh triangles)
  • S: toggle strands (threads)
  • G: toggle branch graph (skeleton)
  • C: toggle contours
  • C: toggle normals
